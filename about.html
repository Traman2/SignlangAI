About HandSign AI

HandSign AI is an innovative project aimed at recognizing hand signs using AI technology. Our goal is to bridge the communication gap for individuals who use sign language.

Our Mission

Our mission is to leverage the power of AI to create accessible and inclusive communication tools for everyone. We believe in the potential of technology to make a positive impact on society.
